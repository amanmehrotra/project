About Us

Our objective is to make a social gathering of alumnus, teachers and students and providing a platform to interact with juniors, seniors and teachers by sharing ideas & experience and foster new ties as well.

A Quick Flashback

The Department of Computer Science was established in the year 1987 with the main objective of providing trained manpower in the area of Computer and Systems Sciences and to promote state of the art research in Computer Science,Applications and related areas.M.Sc. program was started in 1990 and M.C.A. program in 1994.

Our Vision

This portal provides a platform to connect the alumnus with each other and a channel of communication between the students of the department and alumni.It's objective is to make a healthy interaction among alumni,tand students.It helps alumni to be updated with the department news and activities.

Our Team
